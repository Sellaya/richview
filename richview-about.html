About Richview Capital MIC

Experience, governance, and strategic expertise backing every investment decision. Meet the team dedicated to your success.

Our Story

Building trust through expertise and client-focused solutions

Richview Capital Mortgage Investment Corporation is focused on residential mortgage opportunities, filling the gap created by tighter lending guidelines at traditional banks and trust companies. Reputation and trust are earned. Experience solidifies our business foundation time and time again.

Reaching a customer's goal and receiving referrals from them are the measures of our success. We constantly aim for solutions. These ideals define us at Richview Capital.

Guided by corporate governance and strategic knowledge provided by a highly seasoned advisory board, Richview Capital MIC is led by a team of industry experts who have previously managed and guided operations of highly efficient alternative lenders in the Canadian marketplace.

Our Leadership Team

Industry experts driving strategic vision and operational excellence

Guido Di Franco

President and CEO, Founder

Guido's focus is maintaining high quality mortgage portfolios by setting our lending parameters and guidelines, and ensuring our company is constantly adapting to changing dynamics in the industry.

Guido has over 15 years of personal lending experience which brought him extensive knowledge of real estate investing and residential home building.

Guido was a business entrepreneur for over 25 years building start-ups and turning them into successful businesses grossing multi-million dollars in sales. Currently, Guido is a principal broker for a mortgage brokerage with over 10 years of industry experience.

Email Guido

Teresa Di Franco

Executive VP & COO, Co-Founder

Teresa is primarily responsible for business development and day-to-day operations. She holds the oversight on risk and human resources. Teresa has over 30 years of experience in banking, investments and credit adjudication, starting with a productive management career with The Toronto Dominion Bank.

Teresa has won multiple awards in the industry including the prestigious "Women of Influence 2016," from the CMP-Canadian Mortgage Professional Magazine.

Teresa is also an accredited financial planner having earned the Personal Financial Planner (PFP) designation and holds the Accredited Mortgage Professional (AMP) designation.
