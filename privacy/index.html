Privacy Policy

How Richview Capital Corp. collects, uses, and protects your personal information.

Effective Date: January 1, 2025  | Last Updated: March 2025  | Richview Capital Corp., Woodbridge, Ontario

This Privacy Policy applies to Richview Capital Corp. ("Richview Capital," "we," "us," or "our"), a licensed Mortgage Investment Corporation (MIC #13171) registered in Ontario. It governs the collection, use, and disclosure of personal information through our website at richviewcapitalmic.com and through our lending, investing, and brokerage partnership services.

1. Our Commitment to Your Privacy

Richview Capital Corp. is committed to protecting the privacy and confidentiality of all personal information we receive in connection with our services. We comply with the Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act (PIPEDA), the Financial Services Regulatory Authority of Ontario (FSRA) requirements, and all applicable Ontario privacy legislation.

2. What Personal Information We Collect

Depending on how you interact with us, we may collect the following types of personal information:

Identity information: Full name, date of birth, government-issued identification

Contact information: Email address, phone number, mailing address

Financial information: Income, employment status, credit history, assets and liabilities, banking details

Property information: Address, legal description, appraisal details, mortgage history

Investment information: RRSP/RRIF/TFSA account details, investment objectives, risk profile

Website usage data: IP address, browser type, pages visited, time on site, referral sources (collected via cookies and analytics tools)

Communications: Records of your inquiries, emails, and consultation requests

3. How We Collect Your Information

We collect personal information through:

Online inquiry forms and consultation booking requests on our website

Direct communications (phone, email, in-person meetings)

Mortgage applications and supporting documentation submitted by you or your broker

Third-party sources such as credit bureaus, land registry databases, and appraisal firms (with your consent where required)

Automated collection through cookies and web analytics tools when you visit our website

4. How We Use Your Information

We use personal information for the following purposes:

Evaluating and processing mortgage and financing applications

Assessing investor applications and administering MIC investments

Verifying identity and conducting anti-money laundering (AML) and know-your-client (KYC) checks as required by law

Communicating with you about your application, account, or inquiry

Administering loan servicing, payments, and investor distributions

Improving our website and services

Complying with legal, regulatory, and reporting obligations

Sending relevant updates or information about our services (with your consent)

5. Disclosure of Your Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

Service providers: Legal counsel, accountants, appraisers, title insurers, mortgage administrators, and IT providers who assist us in delivering our services, under confidentiality obligations

Regulatory bodies: FSRA, FINTRAC, and other government or regulatory authorities as required by law

Affiliated brokers: With your explicit consent, when your file is submitted by or referred to a licensed mortgage broker

Business transactions: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, subject to confidentiality protections

Legal requirements: Where required by court order, subpoena, or applicable law

6. Marketing Communications and CASL Compliance

Richview Capital Corp. complies with Canada's Anti-Spam Legislation (CASL). We will only send you commercial electronic messages (CEMs), including emails, newsletters, and follow-up communications about our products and services, if we have your express or implied consent to do so.

Express consent: When you submit an inquiry or consultation request through our website forms, you may be asked to indicate whether you wish to receive future marketing communications from us. Consent checkboxes are never pre-checked. You must actively opt in. Your consent, along with the date, time, and manner it was obtained, is recorded and retained.

Implied consent: If you submit an inquiry, application, or consultation request, we may contact you in relation to that specific request under an implied existing business relationship for up to two (2) years from your last transaction or inquiry with us, or six (6) months from an inquiry that did not result in a transaction, in accordance with CASL.

Unsubscribe: Every commercial electronic message we send will include a clear and functional unsubscribe mechanism. We will honour all unsubscribe requests within 10 business days. To opt out at any time, click "Unsubscribe" in any email we send, or contact us at .

Transactional messages, such as confirmation of your inquiry submission, responses to your consultation request, or communications directly related to a mortgage or investment application you have initiated, are not commercial electronic messages under CASL and may be sent without a separate marketing consent.

7. Information Collected Through Our Website Forms

When you submit a form on this Site (inquiry form, consultation booking, or application), we collect the personal information you provide (such as name, email, phone number, and your stated interest). This information is used to:

Respond to your inquiry within our standard 24-hour response window

Assess your eligibility for our lending or investment products

Schedule and conduct consultations you have requested

Comply with KYC, AML, and other regulatory obligations that may arise

Contact you regarding your specific inquiry or related services (under implied consent)

Send marketing communications, if you have provided express consent to do so

By submitting a form on this Site, you acknowledge that you have read this Privacy Policy and consent to the collection and use of your information as described herein for the purpose of responding to your inquiry. Marketing communications are separate and require your express opt-in consent.

8. Cookies and Website Tracking

Our website uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to understand how visitors use our site and to improve user experience. This may include analytics tools such as Google Analytics. You can disable cookies through your browser settings; however, some features of our website may not function properly as a result.

We do not use cookies for targeted advertising or sell browsing data to third-party advertisers.

9. Data Retention

We retain personal information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected, and as required by applicable law and regulation. For mortgage and financial records, this is typically a minimum of seven (7) years following the conclusion of a transaction. Website inquiry data is retained for a reasonable period to address your request and for internal reporting purposes.

10. Security

We implement reasonable physical, administrative, and technical safeguards to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, disclosure, loss, or alteration. These measures include secure data storage, restricted access controls, and confidentiality obligations for all staff and service providers with access to personal data.

While we take reasonable precautions, no method of electronic transmission or storage is completely secure. We encourage you to exercise caution when transmitting sensitive information.

11. Your Rights

Subject to applicable law, you have the right to:

Request access to the personal information we hold about you

Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete information

Withdraw consent to the use of your information for non-essential purposes (subject to legal and contractual limitations)

Request information about how your data is being used

To exercise any of these rights, please contact our Privacy Officer using the contact details below.

12. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites and encourage you to review their privacy policies before submitting any personal information.

13. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. The updated version will be posted on our website with the revised effective date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.

13. AML/KYC and Mandatory Reporting

As of October 11, 2024, Richview Capital Corp. is a reporting entity under the Proceeds of Crime (Money Laundering) and Terrorist Financing Act (PCMLTFA) as a mortgage administrator and lender. We are required by law to collect, verify, and retain identity information from borrowers and investors, and to report certain transactions and suspicious activities to FINTRAC. These obligations override consent requirements under PIPEDA and are non-negotiable conditions of using our services.

14. Transfer of Information Outside Canada

In some cases, personal information we collect may be transferred to, processed, or stored by service providers located outside of Canada (for example, cloud-based software providers). When this occurs, the information may be subject to the laws of the jurisdiction in which it is processed, which may differ from Canadian privacy law. We take reasonable contractual steps to ensure such providers maintain appropriate privacy protections. By using our Site and services, you acknowledge and consent to such transfers where necessary.
