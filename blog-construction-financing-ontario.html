Construction financing: draws and completion

Construction and renovation loans are usually funded in stages (“draws”) as work is completed and verified. That protects both the borrower and the lender. Richview offers construction-related financing in Ontario alongside our other private mortgage solutions.

Typical flow

  • An agreed advance schedule tied to project milestones
  • Documentation or inspections before the next advance
  • A clear plan for completion or exit (for example refinance)
