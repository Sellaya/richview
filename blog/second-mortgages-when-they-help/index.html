Second mortgages: when they help

A second mortgage is a loan secured behind an existing first mortgage. It uses available equity in your property and is often used for renovations, debt consolidation, or other needs our team discusses with Ontario borrowers.

What lenders review

Underwriting typically considers property value, existing loan amounts, and the borrower’s ability to service the debt. Terms vary by situation; nothing on this page is an offer or rate quote.

How Richview fits in

Richview Capital MIC is a licensed Mortgage Investment Corporation offering private mortgage and construction-related solutions across Ontario, with disciplined underwriting. Details of programs appear on our borrowers and services pages.
