Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about private mortgages and our process

How long does the approval process take?

+

Our streamlined approval process typically takes 24-48 hours for initial decisions. Once approved, we can close deals in as little as 7-10 business days, depending on the complexity of the transaction and completion of due diligence requirements.

What types of properties do you finance?

+

We finance residential properties including single-family homes, multi-family residences, custom home construction projects, and development properties across the GTA and Ontario. Each application is evaluated on a case-by-case basis with flexible underwriting criteria.

What are your loan-to-value (LTV) ratios?

+

We typically offer LTV ratios up to 75-80% on residential first mortgages. For construction projects and second mortgages, LTV ratios vary based on the specific project, location, borrower experience, and overall risk profile.

What documentation do I need to apply?

+

Typically, you'll need: property details and appraisal, proof of income, credit report, identification, and any relevant property purchase agreements or construction plans. Our team will provide a complete checklist when you begin your application.

Do you finance construction projects?

+

Yes! We provide construction financing for custom home builds, renovations, and development projects. Funds are typically released in stages based on a pre-approved draw schedule tied to construction milestones.

Can I get approved if the bank turned me down?

+

Absolutely. We specialize in alternative lending solutions for situations where traditional banks say no. We evaluate each application individually, focusing on the property value and your unique situation rather than just credit scores.
